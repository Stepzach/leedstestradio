Luminaries of Light & Space

Luminaries of Light & Space is a group installation on display at Los Angeles International Airport (LAX) featuring some of the most fundamental figures in crafting the ideologies and aesthetics around the genesis of the Los Angeles based art movement, entitled Light & Space. The installation features an array of sculptural works housed within the newly opened Westgate Terminals.

Peter Krasnow: Breathing Joy and Light exhibition soundtrack

This exhibition, drawn from the Skirball Cultural Center’s extensive Krasnow collection includes a soundtrack produced and inspired by Peter Krasnow's work and personal journey.

'David Bowie in the Soviet Union’ exhibition soundtrack

dublab was commissioned by Wende Museum to create the soundtrack of the David Bowie exhibit that is taking place starting April 1st, 2023.

Together On the Air

The Together On the Air web exhibit by ONE Archives Foundation showcases a body of rare audio materials and stories about Radio GLLU, the first bilingual LGBTQIA+ radio program in the United States.

NewMusicBox Guest Editor Series

New Music USA's web magazine residency featuring articles, exclusive mixes, live performances, and more written and produced by dublab.

New Rules Next Week

Ten Rules by Corita Kent have been reimagined as an audio endeavor read by former students and contemporaries of Corita, including artists, community organizers, and thought leaders, providing a personal reflection in their own words.

Deep Routes

Deep Routes is a radio series produced with Metro Art that surveys the intersecting musical histories embedded in the Los Angeles landscape.

Lee Alexander McQueen soundtrack

This LACMA exhibition soundtrack, created by dublab, reflects McQueen’s vast, eclectic, and at times unpredictable musical taste that defined his runway shows.
